Sky City by Oberoi Realty is a new residential project in Mumbai’s Andheri-Dahisar district, located in the booming suburb of Borivali East. Sky City, developed by Oberoi Realty, provides a variety of economical and elegantly constructed apartments and villas, making it a great alternative for homeowners seeking a low-budget yet luxurious living experience.

Sky City is a huge 25-acre development with six skyscrapers now under construction. The project is expected to reinvent the notion of large living, with intelligently built apartments and villas in 3BHK, 4BHK, 6BHK, and 7BHK configurations. Furthermore, magnificent 2BHK villas are offered to meet the different demands and tastes of potential customer

Oberoi Realty’s Sky City is RERA-registered under the registration number P51800003582, assuring transparency and adherence to quality standards. The project is painstakingly designed, both inside and externally, and is slated for completion in December 2023. The development is moving quickly, and a significant number of units have already been sold, demonstrating the project’s attractiveness among homeowners.

Sky City, with its prominent location in Borivali East, provides a great connection to important districts of Mumbai. Residents will have easy access to nearby educational institutions, healthcare services, retail complexes, and entertainment venues. The strategic placement of the project also allows for seamless access to key road networks and transit hubs, making everyday commuting more comfortable

Sky City by Oberoi Realty has an appealing pricing list, with 3BHK apartments beginning at Rs. 3.16 crore, 4BHK apartments starting at Rs. 3.75 crore, and 6BHK apartments starting at Rs. 7.21 crore. Furthermore, the property provides 2 BHK villas beginning at Rs. 79 lakhs. Sky City is an appealing alternative for both end-users and investors due to its affordability and Oberoi Realty’s brand recognition.

Overall, Sky City by Oberoi Realty is the pinnacle of affordable luxury, combining comfort, beauty, and a desirable lifestyle. The project aims to provide a living environment that exceeds expectations and meets the ambitions of its residents, with well-designed houses, extensive green areas, and an assortment of modern facilities.
